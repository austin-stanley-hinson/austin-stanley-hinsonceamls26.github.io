what_i_learned: |
  Today was focused on preparing for the Mid-Summer Symposium. I spent most of
  the day working on our presentation slides and practicing how to clearly explain
  my part of the project.

  A big part of the day also went into preparing my demo video. I recorded several
  repetitions and tried different versions to find the best one. Even though I did
  not feel like I got the perfect take, the process helped me become more familiar
  with the flow of my notebook and what I needed to explain during the presentation.

  Later, we had demo presentations with Groups 1 and 8. Watching the other groups
  present and receiving feedback helped us identify areas where our own presentation
  could improve, especially in terms of clarity, timing, and how we explain our
  results.

blockers: |
  The main challenge today was getting a strong demo video and making sure the
  presentation was polished enough for the symposium. Timing and clarity were
  still areas we needed to improve.

reflection: |
  Today reminded me that presenting research is just as important as doing the
  technical work. Practicing the demo and presenting in front of other groups
  helped me see what needed to be tightened before the symposium. Even though the
  process was stressful, it helped me feel more prepared and aware of what to
  improve.
