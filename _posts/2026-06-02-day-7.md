  limitations of AlphaFold2 and how the use of alanine residues can help improve

  prediction methods and their implications for computational drug discovery.

  additional time discussing literature reviews and how to effectively analyze

  session, I completed my design project, which involved recreating the Netflix

  prototyping, layout design, and applying core UI/UX principles.

  games using Python, Gradio, and the Groq API. The projects helped reinforce my
  understanding of APIs, user interfaces, and how AI models can be integrated
  into interactive applications.
