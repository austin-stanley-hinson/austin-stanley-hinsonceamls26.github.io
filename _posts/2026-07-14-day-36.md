what_i_learned: |
  Today was mostly focused on paper writing. I helped draft and refine the
  Literature Review and Methodology sections of our main research paper. This
  helped me think more carefully about how to connect the background research,
  QSAR modeling, compound ranking, and docking workflow into one clear story.

  I also worked with my teammates on the commercial video for our project. The
  video is meant to introduce our Alzheimer's drug discovery workflow in a more
  creative and accessible way for the symposium. As part of the scene, Jonathan
  acted as an Alzheimer's patient, which made the process both creative and funny
  while still helping us think about how to communicate the purpose of the
  project clearly.

blockers: |
  The main challenge today was balancing the serious nature of Alzheimer's disease
  with the creative tone of the commercial video. We wanted the video to be
  engaging without losing respect for the topic.

reflection: |
  Today helped me see two different sides of research communication. The paper
  required a formal and structured explanation of our work, while the commercial
  video required us to explain the same project in a simple and engaging way.
  Working on both helped me better understand how to present scientific research
  to different audiences.
